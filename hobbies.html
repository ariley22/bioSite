Hobbies

Nicole in her free time

One of the hobbies Nicole is most passionate about is yoga. She trained as a yogi, although she still only does it as a hobby. She relies on different yoga practices constantly when she has any sort of pain or stress.

Nicole is also very passionate about making the world a better place. She started a Peace Club in high school that engaged in various activities. Nowadays she still looks for little ways to make people's lives brighter.

Nicole has been obsessed with Christmas since she was very young. She loves the aesthetic and the way that people are generally cheerier and kinder (although it would be nice if that was the norm). During that time of year you can always find her doing something Christmassy and smiling constantly.
